I am a Cuban-born post-producer and audiovisual editor based in Tampa. I film, edit and post-produce content for Internet and television.

My greatest strength is the passion of expressing emotions through the art of editing and post-production. My greatest pleasure is to create as a team, respecting individual initiative and learning from mistakes.

I master applications such as: Final Cut, Motion, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere, Adobe Illustrator, Sony Vegas, Corel Draw. Sound Technician: Application domain like: FL Studio, Adobe Audition, Cubase, Sony Vegas, and Logic pro.

My work has been showcased at the 'Festival Internacional del Nuevo Cine Latinoamericano' in Cuba and Festival 'Muestra Joven ICAIC' in Havana. In 2016 I directed, photographed and edited Pinero Pinero documentary, which received several awards in 'Muestra Joven ICAIC'.

I have photographed music videos for significant artists and bands such as the rapper Aldo Rodríguez Vaquero, Real Project band and the singer-songwriter Pablo Milanés.

I have been working as a director and cinematographer, editor and post-producer on documentaries, music videos, and fiction short films.

Currently, I'm working in the web series 'Brújula', that analyzes the reconstruction of a dialogue between the Cubans of the island and the Cubans of the exile.

As an artist, I think that creativity is the only tool for individual and collective development.
